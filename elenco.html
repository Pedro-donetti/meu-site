Cast

Lucifer Morningstar

The protagonist. He is the third oldest of all God's angels and the infamous ruler of Hell. He was the ruler of Hell for ages, until he decided he wasn't happy and needed a new beginning.

Chloe Decker

Homicide detective working in Los Angeles as a member of the LAPD. She's assisted by Lucifer on cases (despite her protests).

Amenadiel

Eldest of all of Gods angels, and thus the older brother of Lucifer and all the other angels. Amenadiel has came to Earth countless times to convince Lucifer to return to Hell.

Mazikeen

Maze is among the oldest and most powerful demons who have existed for thousands of years. Lucifer has called her "the most skilled and efficient torturer Hell's ever known".

Dan Espinoza

Homicide detective in Los Angeles, working as a member of the LAPD. Dan dislikes Lucifer quite a bit, due to Lucifer calling him "Detective Douche" and his connection to Dan's ex-wife, Chloe Decker.

Ella Lopez

Her grandmother taught her how to pick locks and she used the skill to steal cars. She later moved to Los Angeles and is now working with LAPDs forensic team.
